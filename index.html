Principal's messager

Aranmore Catholic College has been providing quality Catholic education to the St Mary’s Parish and the Leederville community for over 110 years.

The College is located on a delightful campus in Leederville which has been experiencing an urban renewal for quite some years. The College’s proximity to transport means that it draws students from a wide and disparate area. We have a welcoming approach to all students who might like to attend the College and we offer a broad and rich educational experience. The College has a strong focus on academic achievement and boasts many success stories amongst its alumni.
